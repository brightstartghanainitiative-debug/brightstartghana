About Us

Bright Start Ghana Initiative is a community-led nonprofit based in Gomoa Fetteh, a fishing community in Ghana’s Central Region. We support early childhood education by providing school supplies, nutritious meals, and teacher assistance for 50 pre-KG children at the local DA Pre-KG. Our mission is to give every child a strong educational foundation rooted in love, faith, and opportunity.

Programs

Early Learning Support

Books, play materials, child-friendly classroom setup, and learning mats.

Nutrition & Hygiene

Daily snacks, hygiene kits, and safe drinking water support to improve attendance and health.

Teacher Support

Stipends, training in early-childhood pedagogy, and volunteer mentorship.

Parent Engagement

Workshops and materials to empower parents as first teachers.

How You Can Help

Your support helps us deliver direct, measurable benefits to children in Gomoa Fetteh. Suggested ways to help:

  • Donate learning materials or funds for supplies
  • Sponsor meals or support the feeding program
  • Partner with our local teachers for training
  • Share our story with churches and community groups

Suggested project budget (initial): $2,300 — learning materials, teacher support, feeding for 50 children (3 months), community sessions.
