Our Mission

Bright Start Ghana Initiative is a community-led nonprofit dedicated to giving young children in rural Ghana a fair and strong start in life through access to quality early-childhood education, learning resources, and teacher support.

Why Gomoa Fetteh?

Gomoa Fetteh is a fishing community in Ghana's Central Region where poverty levels remain high. Many families struggle to provide educational support for their children, especially during the crucial pre-kindergarten years (ages 3-5). The local DA Pre-KG classroom faces critical shortages:

  • No age-appropriate books or learning materials
  • Limited play equipment for early development
  • Teacher lacks training in early childhood education
  • No feeding program - children attend school hungry
  • Parents have no resources to support learning at home

The result? Children start primary school unprepared, struggle academically, and often drop out early - perpetuating the cycle of poverty.
